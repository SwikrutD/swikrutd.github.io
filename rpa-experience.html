Featured Automations

Production Reporting  ·  Daily · Mon–Fri

Late Press Titles Report

A fully automated reporting pipeline that monitors an email inbox for a daily production data extract, applies business logic to filter out irrelevant records (future ship dates and on-hold jobs), consolidates duplicate entries by summing remaining hours, and produces a formatted, pivot-ready Excel report — all delivered to production stakeholders before the workday begins.

IMAP Email Excel / XLSX Google Sheets Pivot Tables Data Filtering

Vendor Management  ·  Scheduled

MVP1 PO Follow-Ups

Automates the entire purchase order follow-up workflow — from logging into a web-based plant management system and pulling an overdue PO line items report, to grouping results by vendor, looking up contact emails, and dispatching personalized follow-up emails with vendor-specific Excel attachments. Handles edge cases where vendor contact data is missing by notifying plant contacts directly, ensuring no vendor falls through the cracks.

Chrome Automation Web App Interaction Excel Export Per-Vendor Emails Exception Handling

Logistics Reporting  ·  Hourly · Queue-Based

LogPro Reports – Dispatcher & Performer

A scalable two-bot architecture built on UiPath's ReFramework with Orchestrator queues. The Dispatcher reads a Google Sheet config to determine which of 17 billing and logistics report types to run, and calculates dynamic date ranges (Prior Day, Month-to-Date, Prior Week, and custom work day logic). The Performer processes each queue item by connecting to a legacy AS400 terminal, selecting the correct report, and routing outputs to the right recipients — with full transaction-level error handling and automated failure alerts.
